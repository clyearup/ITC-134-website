The Use of Technology in the Current State of the World

Technology has been known to make teaching & learning easier. The world is in the middle of a pandemic; the education and professional world has been relying heavily on technology in order to maintain the teaching/learning process. One of the main ways that schools have taken advantage is video calls. The most popular application that is currently being used world-wide for this purpose is Zoom. This has proved to be the best and safest way to go about maintaining the education process. Pros & Cons

While the online learning process seems to be going smoothly, there are still problems faced by both the school that have had to switch and the students that are having to adapt to this new and hopefully not permanent way. Some of these problems include not having access to a great internet service, devices such as webcam computers, problems with different learning mechanisms such as knowledge assessments. Below are some pros and cons of this transition:

Pros:
  • Develops self learning skills
  • Helps develop independence
  • Might offer a more flexible schedule
  • Helps develop computer use skills
Cons:
  • Strain on the eye from too much screen time
  • Little or no face to face interaction
  • Intense self discipline and direction required
