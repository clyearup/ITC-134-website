The use of technology in relation to the current state of the world. Technology has been known to make the teaching/learning process easier and smooth going. The world is in the middle of a pandemic at this moment and the education and professional world has been relying heavily on more recent technology in order to maintain the teaching/learning process. One of the main ways that schools have taken advantage of is video calls. The most popular application that is currently being used world-wide for this purpose is Zoom. This by far has proved to be the best and safest way to go about maintaining the education process.
